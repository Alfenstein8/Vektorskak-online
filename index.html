Spectators

How to play

In the default game mode you have three chains that you control.
If you are on team blue you will control the blue chains and wise-versa.
A chains can consist of up to three joints.
The fist joint is the head. This is the one that you can move.
The second is the neck/heart. This is your weak spot. Where an enemy chain can attack.
And the last is the tail.
If you get hit in the neck/heart. You’ll be sent back to your tail. If you don’t have a tail you’ll be sent back to the start.
To win you have to place one of your chains head on the enemies base, marked in their color.
Good luck!
